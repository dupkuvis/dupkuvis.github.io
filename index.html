About Me

I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate of Center for Information Science, School of EECS, Peking University. I am now in Peking University Visualization and Visual Computing Lab. My research focuses on scientific visualization, including flow visualization, ensemble visualization, and high performance visualization.
For more details, please see my curriculum vitae in English or 中文.
